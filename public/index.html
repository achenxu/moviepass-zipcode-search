What is MoviePass?

MoviePass is a subscription service based out of the United States that allows subscribers to pay a flat monthly fee to see as many movies as they'd like in a month -- up to one a day.

How much does it cost?

MoviePass currently charges $9.99/mo for it's service.

How does it work?

MoviePass sends you something similar to a debit card. You download their app, check in at the theater you wish to purchase the ticket from, and then the card is loaded with the funds needed to purchase tickets.
